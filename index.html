About

For as long as I can remember, I have been fascinated by photographs.


From the heart warming tears of a father when his daughter becomes a bride to the awe inspiring moment when the morning Sun makes an appearance in the Himalayan landscape. Our ability to preserve an instance in time, to capture light from a moment and convert it into an everlasting memory, I believe, is nothing short of magic.


I have pursued this magic in all its forms. From directing films to travelling with photography lovers around the country, weddings to historical monuments, portraits to events, product to fashion spreads, I am excited to frame them all, I enjoy lighting them all and I love to shoot them all.


I think myself to be extremely fortunate that photography, which was my childhood obsession, turned into passion has now become my full time profession.


- Kritika -
